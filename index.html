About Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra, located in the heart of Haryana, India, is a sacred and historic land known for the epic battle of the Mahabharata. It is the divine setting where Lord Krishna narrated the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna.

The city is dotted with revered temples, sacred lakes like Brahma Sarovar, and spiritual landmarks such as Jyotisar. It's not only a pilgrimage destination but also a center of learning and heritage.
